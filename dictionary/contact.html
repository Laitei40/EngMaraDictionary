Contact Us

We welcome questions, feedback, corrections, and contributions. Whether you have found an error in a definition, want to suggest a new word, or simply want to learn more about the Mara language, we would love to hear from you.

Get in Touch

The easiest way to reach us is by opening an issue or starting a discussion on our GitHub repository. This keeps communication open and allows the community to participate.

Open a GitHub Issue Start a Discussion

Suggest a Word or Correction

Found a translation that is inaccurate, incomplete, or missing? You can suggest improvements directly on any word’s detail page using the “Suggest improvement” button. All suggestions are reviewed by our team before being published.

Search the Dictionary

Response Time

This project is maintained on a voluntary basis. We aim to respond to all queries and review all suggestions within a reasonable timeframe, though response times may vary. We appreciate your patience and your contributions to preserving the Mara language.

Data & Privacy Inquiries

For questions related to your personal data, data deletion requests, or privacy concerns, please open a GitHub issue and label it privacy. We will address your request as promptly as possible. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
