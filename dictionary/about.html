A comprehensive English ⇄ Mara dictionary

Discover and learn the Mara (Lakher) language through an online dictionary that provides practical definitions, translations, and example sentences. MaraDict is designed to preserve and promote the Mara language — a Kuki-Chin language spoken primarily in Mizoram (India) and Chin State (Myanmar).

Aim & Objective

The primary aim is to serve as an online dictionary, providing practical and meaningful definitions for the Mara language. We are committed to continually expanding the vocabulary, ensuring ease of use and relevance for all users across various devices. Our goal is to make the dictionary accessible on all modern devices, catering to both beginner and advanced learners.

How It Works

  • Search — Type any English or Mara word. Results appear instantly as you type.
  • Direction — Toggle between English → Mara and Mara → English.
  • Browse — Explore words alphabetically using the A–Z browser.
  • Shareable Links — Every word has a permanent link you can share.
  • Offline Ready — Previously searched words are cached for offline access.

Contribute

To ensure that MaraDict continues to grow and provide comprehensive definitions, we rely on contributions from the community. If you come across any errors, have new words to suggest, or want to improve existing definitions, we welcome your input.
